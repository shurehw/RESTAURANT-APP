# How to Install OpSOS Pulse on Your Phone

Pulse gives you **real-time sales tracking** right from your home screen — no app store needed.

---

## iPhone (iOS)

1. Open **Safari** (must be Safari — Chrome won't work for this)
2. Go to: **`opsos-restaurant-app.vercel.app`**
3. Log in with your email and password
4. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
5. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
6. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
7. Pulse is now on your home screen — open it like any other app

> **Tip:** If you don't see "Add to Home Screen", scroll right in the share menu — it may be hidden.

---

## Android

1. Open **Chrome**
2. Go to: **`opsos-restaurant-app.vercel.app`**
3. Log in with your email and password
4. You should see a banner at the bottom saying **"Add Pulse to Home screen"** — tap **Install**
5. If no banner appears: tap the **three-dot menu** (top right) and select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**
6. Tap **Install**
7. Pulse is now on your home screen

---

## What You Get

- **Live Pulse** — real-time revenue and covers vs. forecast during service
- **Nightly Report** — end-of-day sales, comps, and operational summary
- **Attestations** — view previous nightly submissions
- Works offline for recently viewed data
- Updates automatically in the background

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| "Add to Home Screen" not showing (iPhone) | Make sure you're using **Safari**, not Chrome or another browser |
| App opens in browser instead of standalone | Remove it from home screen and re-add using the steps above |
| Can't log in | Check your email/password, or ask your manager to verify your account is set up |
| Data looks stale | Pull down to refresh, or close and reopen the app |

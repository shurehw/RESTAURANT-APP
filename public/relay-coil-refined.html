Relay Coil — Refined

Same concept (electromagnetic coil + switch), four different executions. Optimized for legibility at small sizes.

E1. Zigzag Coil

Coil as clean zigzag between two terminals. Reads like a schematic symbol. The switch arm extends from center. Most legible at small sizes.

E3. Horizontal Solenoid

Coil runs horizontal with bumps on top. More compact, balanced aspect ratio. Switch arm drops down from right terminal. Feels more like an engine component.

E4. Boxed Coil

Standard relay schematic: coil represented as a box with diagonal line, two terminals. The most technically correct electrical relay symbol. Clean, recognizable to anyone who's read a circuit diagram.
